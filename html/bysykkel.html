Bergen bysykkel - bike rent

Bergen bysykkel is rentable non-motorized bikes that are available in a number of different locations in the Bergen area.

Bikes are rentable from 05:00 to 24:00 with the use of the Bergen Bysykkel app. The rented bike needs to be returned to one of the many available bike stations after use. For a full guide and map over stations see Bergenbysykkel.no or by downloading the app. Pricing here varies between 399 NOK for a yearly pass, 79 NOK for a monthly subscription (subscription is automatically renewed at 149 NOK unless cancelled), 49 NOK for a day pass or 29 NOK for a single trip. These prices include unlocking the bike and a 45 minute ride. After 45 minutes they charge 5 NOK for every 15 minutes. More information can be found at the Bergen Bysykkels website

When do you choose to travel by Bergen Bysykkel?

I use Bergen Bysykkel when it makes sense in terms of where I am going. You have to park the bicycles at a dedicated Bergen Bysykkel station, so when I am heading to a place where one is close it is a preferable means of transportation for me.

What is the best thing about traveling by Bergen Bysykkel compared to other available transportation options?

You can get a year pass for 399 NOK which is almost the same amount I would have to pay for a monthly pass on bus and Bybanen. Cycling also is a refreshing feeling and starts the day.

Bergen Bysykkel - a good option for seeing attractions near the city center

We recommend Bergen Bysykkel for use if you are going somewhere close to a Bergen Bysykkel station, as you can find on the Bergen Bysykkel interactive map. We therefore see Bergen Bysykkel as a good option for going to attractions in or around the city center.
